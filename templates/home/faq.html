FAQ

1How Can I use Wanna Chat?

Visit the Wanna Chat site, then click "Start Chat" after filling out the form. The option of becoming a VIP member is also available. To sign up as a VIP member, visit https://www.Wanna Chat.us/user/signup form.

2 Sending a message to an online user, how is it done?

Following your login to Wanna Chat chat, you will see a list of everyone who is now online on your left. Click the name of the person you wish to talk with to bring up the chat window, where you may type your message before clicking the "send" button to send it.

3How safe is Wanna Chat?

You should always check the safety advice page before you take any action after joining Wanna Chat. To ensure safety, the Wanna Chat Development Team and Administration have taken extra precautions by having the option to restrict certain nations and IPs with a poor reputation.

4 If I have a problem, how can I contact your back office support?

When you have a problem, you may definitely visit our contact page and get in touch with us. We are here to listen to you and to assist you in solving any issue. Our contact page may be reached directly at https://www.Wanna Chat.us/user/contactus.

5 What kind of individuals are here in Wanna Chat?

Men and women from all over the world may be found and encountered who want to build relationships, meet new people, or discover that special someone.
